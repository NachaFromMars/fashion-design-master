# fashion-design-master — Master-level fashion and uniform design

> Design team uniforms, brand apparel, and merch with master-level fashion knowledge. Informed by Hermès, Off-White, Balenciaga, Supreme, Yohji Yamamoto, and 15+ global brands.

[![OpenClaw Skill](https://img.shields.io/badge/OpenClaw-Skill-blueviolet)](https://github.com/NachaFromMars)

## Overview
fashion-design-master is a fashion design knowledge skill covering brand analysis, color theory, typography on fabric, logo placement, garment design, and production quality control. It encodes 10 core design insights and 15 golden rules drawn from studying leading luxury and streetwear houses. Use it for designing team uniforms, company merch, brand apparel, or any fashion-related creative brief.

## Features
**10 Core Insights** (from Off-White, Yohji, Demna, Hermès, Supreme, etc.)
- Quotation marks as design language
- Deconstruction over decoration
- Black as statement
- Typography as silent voice
- Logo placement as architecture
- And 5 more

**15 Golden Rules** covering:
- Logo placement hierarchy (chest 3-4" / sleeve 1-2" / back / collar), golden ratio sizing
- Color & contrast (WCAG 4.5:1 minimum, Pantone PMS for production, no navy/black clash)
- Fabric & construction (weight vs. logo size, oversized proportions)
- Production (embroidery vs. print minimums, woven label craft)
